Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our pricing

Are there any hidden fees?

No, our pricing is completely transparent. The price you see is the price you pay for the services listed in each package. If additional services are requested beyond the scope of the package, we'll provide a clear quote before proceeding.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for all our packages. Typically, we require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. Please contact us to discuss options that work for your budget.

What happens after the support period ends?

After the included support period ends, you can purchase additional support on an as-needed basis or opt for one of our monthly maintenance packages. Our maintenance packages include regular updates, security monitoring, content updates, and technical support.

Do your packages include hosting fees?

Yes — hosting is fully included in all of our packages. We'll take care of setup and deployment so your website is live and accessible without any extra fees.

What if I need changes after the revision rounds are used?

Additional revision rounds can be purchased as needed. We charge a reasonable hourly rate for changes requested beyond the included revision rounds. We'll always provide a clear estimate before proceeding with any additional work.

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline varies depending on the package and specific requirements. Generally, our Starter package takes 2-4 weeks, Professional takes 4-6 weeks, and Premium takes 6-8 weeks. Custom projects may take longer depending on complexity. We'll provide a specific timeline during our initial consultation.
