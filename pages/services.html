Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline for a website project depends on its complexity and scope. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex sites with custom functionality may take 6-8 weeks or longer. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a more specific timeline based on your project requirements.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes, we offer website maintenance services to keep your site secure, up-to-date, and running smoothly. This includes regular updates, security monitoring, content updates, and technical support. We can customize a maintenance plan based on your specific needs.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All websites we create are fully responsive and mobile-friendly by default. We test extensively on various devices and screen sizes to ensure your website looks and functions perfectly for all users, regardless of how they access it.

Do you provide content for the website?

While we primarily focus on design and development, we can provide basic content guidance and structure. For comprehensive content creation, we can recommend professional copywriters who specialize in web content. We're happy to collaborate with your existing content team or copywriters as well.

How much input will I have in the design process?

You'll have significant input throughout the design process. We begin by understanding your preferences, brand guidelines, and vision. We then present design concepts for your feedback and make revisions based on your input. Our goal is to create a website you love and that effectively represents your business.

What if I need changes after the website launches?

We provide a 30-day support period after launch for minor adjustments and fixes at no additional cost. For more substantial changes or ongoing updates, we offer maintenance packages or can bill hourly for specific changes as needed. We're committed to ensuring your website continues to meet your needs even after launch.
